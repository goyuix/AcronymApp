1. Login to outlook.office365.com
  2. Click the gear icon at the top right and choose Manage add-ins
  3. Click the plus sign and then choose Add from URL: https://goyuix.github.io/AcronymApp/AcronymAppManifest.xml

Want to install an add-in for your entire organization? Just visit https://outlook.office.com/ecp/ and choose add-ins under the organization settings. Too easy!

Note: You may need to access the old version of the options pane due to an upgrade happening on the week of 25-April-2016. You can find it here: https://outlook.office.com/ecp/?rfr=owa&owaparam=modurl%3D0
